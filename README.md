# Outdoor-Exposure-and-Malaria-Positivity-Research-Project
This readme file provides information on the research project titled "Outdoor Exposure Pathways and Malaria Risk in Urban Settings". 
The project aims to assess the relationship between outdoor activities and malaria positivity in urban areas, while examining the contextual influence of socio-economic and demographic characteristics and the role of malaria preventive measures in shaping residual transmission. 

# Description of dataset
The dataset you will be working with comprises survey data collected from pregnant women in two urban  areas during the wet season in Nigeria. 
It captures information on respondentsâ€™ demographic and socioeconomic characteristics, employment and occupational patterns, daily activities including work and leisure, household chores and malaria prevention practices. The dataset also contains malaria test results obtained using the Rapid Diagnostic Test(RDT) Kits. 

## Data Structure
The data has 3,831 observations, with 109 columns and has six main categories of data. Below are the categories with the variable names 
1) __Socio-demographic Variables:__ 
   age_years , dob , education , postsec_edu , marital_status, employed,
   occupation, occupation_other, agri_work_type 
   
2) __Reproductive History Variables__ 
   gest_age_weeks ,  gravidity , parity 

3) __Malaria Prevention Measures__ 
   hh_owns_net, hh_no_net_reason , n_slept_under_net , slept_under_net,
   window_door_screens, ever_used_spray , spray_used_2wks , ever_used_coils,
   coil_used_2wks , other_prev_measures , other_prev_specify ,sp_taken_pregnancy 
   
4) __Outdoor Work Related Expsoure Variables (Times Exposed)__
   outdoor_work_hours_6am_9am , outdoor_work_hours_9am_12noon ,
   outdoor_work_hours_12noon_3pm , outdoor_work_hours_3pm_6pm ",
   outdoor_work_hours_6pm_9pm , outdoor_work_hours_9pm_12am ,
   outdoor_work_hours_12am_3am , outdoor_work_hours_3am_6am
 
5) __Outdoor Recreational/Leisure Related Exposure Variables (Times Exposed)__
   outdoor_leisure_hours_6am_9am ,  outdoor_leisure_hours_9am_12noon ,
   outdoor_leisure_hours_12noon_3pm , outdoor_leisure_hours_3pm_6pm ,
   outdoor_leisure_hours_6pm_9pm , outdoor_leisure_hours_9pm_12am ,
   outdoor_leisure_hours_12am_3am , outdoor_leisure_hours_3am_6am 
   
6) __Outdoor Household Chores Related Exposure Variables (Times Exposed)__
   does_outdoor_chores, outdoor_chores_time_6am_9am, outdoor_chores_time_9am_12noon,
   outdoor_chores_time_12noon_3pm , outdoor_chores_time_3pm_6pm,
